Why I paint

Reclaiming a future where our daughters see themselves whole.

I was raised amid the bougainvillea bloom of Edenfield in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. Those colours echo through my studio in Southern Sweden where I paint to protect slow time and deep presence.

When the world feels loud with distortion, I return to the canvas. Layer by layer, I build spiritual filters that reveal feminine stories.
