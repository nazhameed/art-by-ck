Artist Statement

Cecilia K is a Zimbabwean-born artist based in Southern Sweden. Her work explores identity, motherhood, and ancestral memory through layered Pop Art techniques.

Each piece invites the viewer to slow down, discover hidden motifs, and connect with the lineage that shapes us all. Expect tactile materials, bold colour blocks, and cinematic lighting that changes with every viewing angle.

Mediums
Acrylic, ink, resin, textiles, light
Exhibitions
Malmo, London, Cape Town, virtual residencies
Available For
Gallery shows, commissions, immersive activations
